ABOUT US

PASSION AND POTENTIAL.

NIKE isn’t one product. NIKE is a mindset. It’s a do-more, go-further attitude about life. It’s about pushing limits and breaking boundaries. It’s about creating the future. And you can find it on the court, or on the street, because it’s the backbone of each brand in our family — Nike, Converse, and Jordan.

CREATING THE FUTURE OF SPORT

At NIKE, Inc., we see a world where everybody is an athlete—united in the joy of movement. Driven by our passion for sport and our instinct for innovation, we aim to elevate human potential. Whether our job is designing the ultimate sneaker or coding a revolutionary app, we’re united by the same mission: to bring inspiration and innovation to every athlete* in the world. That means you.

A TRIBUTE TO GREATNESS

What began as an homage to one player—Michael Jordan—has become synonymous with peak performance and an endless pursuit of greatness. Employees in design, merchandising, product development, communications, marketing, sports marketing, and sales bring creativity, innovation and audacious design to footwear and apparel, all with one mission in mind: shape the future of basketball culture on and off the court with the next generation.
